About

Hire from the best institute specialising in Optics and Photonics. Since it's establishment in 1995, International School of Photonics has produced some of the finest young researchers and technicians in the field of Photonics.
ISP is known for its excellence in teaching, research and services to the scientific community. The course structure is unique as it is the only institute in India providing undergraduate five year integrated course in Photonics.
The students are exposed To an up to date curriculum and finest laboratory facilities. Currently ISP has more than 150 students, out of which 100 are MSc( 5 year integrated) students, 36 M.Tech students, 40 PhD students and 4 post doctoral fellows.
