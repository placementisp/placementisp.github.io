About

Hire from the best institute that specialize in Optics and cutting edge technologies. Since its founding in 1995, the International School of Photonics has grown to become one of the flagship, known for the excellence of its teaching, its research, and its service to national and international
communities. International School of Photonics is the only Institute in India which offers an opening in core photonic technology immediately after higher secondary studies. ISP currently has more than 180 studentsof whom 100 are M.Sc ( 5 year Integrated), 36  are M.Tech (Optoelectronics and Laser Technology), 40 are PhD students and 4 Post Doctoral Fellows.
